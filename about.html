College Admission Predictor System is a web based application system in which students can login their marks along with their personal information and list of colleges will be displayed on the screen. This helps to predict their admissions in colleges. Administrator can add the college details and the batch details. The total time for the entrance allotment becomes lower and the allotment process becomes faster. It helps students to make right decisions for choosing their college. In which students can login with their personal as well as marks details to prediction the admission in colleges and the administrator can allot the seats for the students. Using this Application, the entrance seat allotment became easier and can be implemented using system. The main advantage of the project is the computerization of the entrance seat allotment process. Administrator has the power for the allotment. It helps student for making decision for choosing a right college.
